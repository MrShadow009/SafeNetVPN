SECTION 3: ENCRYPTION & PRIVACY FEATURES
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
VPN ENCRYPTION:
- ProtonVPN uses AES-256 encryption.
- Key exchange via 4096-bit RSA handshake.
- Forward secrecy enabled through Perfect Forward Secrecy (PFS).

VPN TUNNELING PROTOCOLS:
- Features OpenVPN (UDP/TCP) and WireGuard (fast, modern).
- Comparison: OpenVPN = widely supported, slower; WireGuard = faster, lean codebase.

SECTION 4: BENEFITS & LIMITATIONS OF VPN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Benefits:
- Masks IP address, hides browsing activity.
- Protects data from ISPs and eavesdroppers on public Wi-Fi.
- Helps bypass censorship and geo-blocks.

Limitations:
- Slower internet connection due to encryption overhead.
- Not complete anonymity - VPN provider can still see traffic.
- Some websites block known VPN IP ranges.
- Free plans may restrict speed, servers, or data usage.
